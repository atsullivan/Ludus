Ludus

In ancient Roman culture, the word Ludus had several meanings. It most famously referred to the training school of gladiators. They were infamous for there rigorous training and extreme discipline. The Ludus is where men would turn into warriors.

Easy to use

Ludus was created because of the need for a simple, intuitive application that can benefit any athlete, beginner or elite. Basic features such as WOD logging and goal setting will make this feel like a fast and easy log book, with added features such as improvement over time.

Ludus is not meant to be the focus of an athletes trainging, but serve as the essential tool that they need to reach their ultimate potential.

All the features you need

With all the applications out there to track fitness, they become overloaded with unecessary features but seem to leave out simple ones. Ludus provides all features that are necessary to modern athletes without overcomplicating the system.

Features

Log daily workouts

Workouts can be stored in your profile, where you can go back to view or edit them. Set up a schedule or log a workout when you want to.

Set goals with time limits

You can create goals for yourself. Set them with a goal date of accomplishment or as a long term goal. Once you complete one, it will be added to your list of met goals.

Track your progress over time

Ludus stores all of your workouts, goals, and PRs. Over time you will be able to see your progress, the best form of motivation.
